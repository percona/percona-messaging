# Solution Bundles

Percona offers a set of focused, fixed-scope solution bundles designed to deliver fast, measurable outcomes using the same engagement models described earlier: Expert Support, ExpertOps, and Expert Consulting and Services. These bundles represent our most common, high-impact use cases, and are based on what customers ask for most frequently.

These bundles are **not** the only way Percona services can be combined or delivered; customers can tailor any engagement by adding Support, ExpertOps, or Consulting modules as needed. The fixed-scope bundles below simply provide a simplified path to achieve results.

| Bundle                                                                                                                        | Outcomes delivered                                                                                       | What's included                                                                                                                                                       |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Performance Optimization Bundle:** for teams needing measurable performance gains or relief from chronic performance issues | Better workload performance, reduced latency and bottlenecks, stabilized systems under load              | Performance tuning engagement (Consulting), proactive health assessment (Consulting), 6 months of Advanced Support, optional add-ons: architecture review or training |
| **QuickStart Bundle:** for new customers validating Percona or quickly stabilizing a smaller deployment                       | Rapid environment stabilization, baseline health and readiness, faster time-to-value with Percona        | Targeted health check or tuning (Consulting), 3 months of Advanced Support, limited to one technology and ≤10 servers                                                 |
| **AI Readiness Bundle:** for teams adopting vector search or preparing PostgreSQL for AI-driven applications                  | PostgreSQL readiness for vector search and AI workloads, better observability, indexing, and concurrency | Setup and tuning of pgvector, pgvectorscale, pg_stat_kcache, pg_wait_sampling, query pattern optimization, observability and performance enhancements                 |
