# Percona ExpertOps

**Proactive, hands-on database management partnership for teams that want Percona to operate part or all of their day-to-day operations.**

ExpertOps is Percona's managed operations offering. Unlike Expert Support, which is reactive and advisory, ExpertOps is proactive and operational, with Percona engineers performing work directly. Percona Experts handle continuous monitoring, tuning, patching, automation, backup validation, and routine operational tasks, using PMM for observability plus Operators and automation frameworks for execution to keep environments stable, predictable, and well-maintained. When scoped and hours are available, ExpertOps can also execute defined changes such as upgrades or migrations, not just advise on them.

- **Who ExpertOps is for:** Teams with limited staff or time to manage databases, companies that need 24×7 operational coverage, organizations that want to prevent incidents instead of reacting to them, environments where uptime is business-critical.
- **Problems ExpertOps solves:** Operational noise and alert fatigue, manual or inconsistent scaling, maintenance and tuning, downtime from preventable issues, operational backlog, and staffing limitations, "day two" operational risk after migrations or upgrades, including inconsistent backup behavior and policy drift across environments.
- **Outcomes ExpertOps delivers:** Proactive issue prevention, stable and predictable performance, consistent tuning and maintenance, reduced operational burden on engineering teams, and faster incident response from engineers already familiar with the environment and its telemetry.
