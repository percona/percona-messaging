# Percona Messaging

**This is the approved source of truth for Percona messaging.**

What you read here is built directly from the [percona-messaging](https://github.com/percona/percona-messaging) repository on GitHub. When messaging is updated and merged to `main`, this site reflects those changes. There is no separate copy to maintain.

## Who this is for

Product managers, Solutions Marketing, field teams, partners, and anyone who needs current positioning, product language, and naming guidance without browsing a GitHub file tree.

## Where to start

- **Company positioning:** [Executive summary](/framework/executive-summary.md), [Core positioning](/framework/core-positioning.md), [Why Percona](/framework/why-percona.md)
- **Value pillars:** cost, performance, security, and future readiness under **Value pillars** in the sidebar
- **Services:** Expert Support, ExpertOps, and Consulting under **Offerings**
- **Products:** MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and others under **Products**
- **Naming rules:** [Canonical naming](/reference/canonical-naming.md) and [Brand voice](/reference/brand-voice.md)

Use **Search** (top of sidebar) to find a topic across the site.

## Propose a change

Messaging improves through pull requests in GitHub. See the [Contributing guide](https://github.com/percona/percona-messaging/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) or [open an issue](https://github.com/percona/percona-messaging/issues/new/choose).
